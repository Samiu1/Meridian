# Workspace: demo

## Product vision
A collaboration tool for distributed product teams that turns scattered
feedback into prioritized, evidence-backed roadmaps.

## North star metric
Weekly active workspaces that ship at least one roadmap update.

## Conventions & terminology
- "Initiative" = quarter-level bet; "Feature" = sprint-level deliverable.
- PRDs use P0/P1/P2 priority labels.
- Tracker project key: DEMO
