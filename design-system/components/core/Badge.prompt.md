A small uppercase pill for status chips and live-state labels; `soft` tinted chips or a raised `neu` pebble pill with an optional pulsing dot.

```jsx
<Badge look="neu" tone="primary" dot pulse>Deep Synthesis Active</Badge>
<Badge tone="success" dot>Optimal</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral`, `primary`, `sage`, `success`, `warning`, `danger`, `info`. Looks: `soft`, `neu`. Props: `dot`, `pulse`.
